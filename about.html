About Me

This is what I do.

I am currently a senior at Florida Atlantic University and I'm so excited to be graduating in May 2020! I've loved living in Boca Raton for the past 3 years but I originally was born and raised in Orlando, Florida. Both my parents are from Port-au-Prince, Haiti which takes everyone by surprise. So I technically speak three languages; English, French, and Creole! Although I understand the languages better than I speak it, it's a great quality to have being in South Florida with such a diverse culture.

I've been involved with my university through the women's soccer club which is something I've done for years and was so happy to be able to continue playing in college for fun. Getting involved has really helped me make friends and connections through out my college experience.

I am also very passionate about travelling! I believe everyone should take the opportunity to travel outside of the country if that opportunity presents itself. I studied abroad last Spring semester in Paris, France and travelled to many different countries and cities. It's such an eye opener to the rest of the world and I'm eager to graduate so I can continue visting these amazing places.
